About

RAAKI CREW in Chennai,,
Tamilnadu

RAAKI Crew provides superior consulting services for managing the events for personal as well as corporate events. RAAKI Crew brings a fresh and innovative approach to end to end event management services, right from planning to execution Our goal is to exceed the expectations of every client by offering outstanding customer service, increased flexibility, and greater value, thus optimizing system functionality and improving operation efficiency. Our associates are distinguished by their functional and technical expertise combined with their hands-on experience, thereby ensuring that our clients receive the most effective and professional service


As experts in event management services, RAAKI Crew is involved in every stage from implementation to completion offering continual, functional and technical support. Our extensive skills encompass all aspects of implementation and operation, including events requirements definition, development of functional specifications.

Our team is well versed in all aspects of event management, from budgeting to production to event coordination and vendor management. We pride ourselves on our proven track record for effectively administering a large number of events. In addition, RAAKI CREW has experience with big corporates as well as personal events. Our specialities lies in Product Launches, Conferences, AGMs, Wedding Events, Artist Management, Manpower Provider and Stage Performances.




For us, every event is different, and we strive to bring our creativity and talent to ensure an experience that uniquely fits your style and taste.

Our approach to events is modern and fresh, free from outdated techniques, cheesy, uncomfortable games, goofy props, and pre-determined sets and playlists. Different from the big multi-op DJ companies who focus on booking as many clients as possible and providing the “cookie-cutter” style DJ, our DJs deliver a different, more personable approach we know our clients appreciate.
